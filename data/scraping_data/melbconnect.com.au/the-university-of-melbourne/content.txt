Melbourne Connect | The University of Melbourne

The University of Melbourne Interdisciplinary Groups
Melbourne Connect is home to a large range of interdisciplinary groups from The University of Melbourne. These groups combine leading University research and real-world applications to create solutions to some of the world's most pressing issues. 
From progressing the science of climate change solutions to better understanding the ins and outs of human psychology through data science, these groups play host to some of the world's leading researchers and thought-leaders.

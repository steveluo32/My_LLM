Melbourne Connect | The Lofts

The Lofts is the University of Melbourne's newest, state-of-the-art graduate student accommodation located in Melbourne Connect. The Lofts is exclusive to University of Melbourne students and has been uniquely designed with students in mind, offering a central location metres from our Parkville campus. 
Residents can enjoy close proximity to the iconic food and shopping district of Lygon Street and access to support services, including an alumni career skills and employability program. The Lofts is also an ideal place to stay for visiting academics and fellows.

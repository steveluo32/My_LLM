Melbourne Connect | Telstra Creator Space

Telstra Creator Space is an accessible fabrication lab located in The University of Melbourne’s innovation precinct, Melbourne Connect. Operated by the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, it’s a space where technology and creativity combine to form innovative solutions. Telstra Creator Space is open to all students and staff at The University of Melbourne and we are planning to welcome Melbourne Connect Co-located partners in the near future. If you are a Melbourne Connect partner, please register your interest at telstra-creatorspace@unimelb.edu.au.Take a virtual tour here.

Student industry projects: School of Computing and Information Systems, The University of Melbourne

Student Industry Projects are unpaid projects undertaken by Masters students in their final year, in collaboration with an industry supervisor and an academic supervisor at the University. Organisations can work with an individual student or a small group, for either one or two academic semesters, depending on the project.

Get a head start on your career.





 







Develop your employability while you study.
Whether you’re exploring your employment options, crafting your resume and cover letter, or applying for an internship—we’re here to equip you with the services you need to develop your career.
Learn more about our services









Discover your career options
Find a career path that best suits your skills, lifestyle, and area of study.





Get career ready
Learn about the skills and experience you need to achieve your goals.





Find a job
Access casual, full-time, and graduate opportunities by visiting Careers Online.





Starting your new job
Get tips on starting your first industry role.





Explore our online platforms




EmployMe
Use EmployMe to find job market reports based on your study area, practice interviews, take career assessments and aptitude tests, and access a range of career learning resources. 





Resume Reviewer: Smart Resume
Our Smart Resume tool uses artificial intelligence to provide real-time insights and feedback on your resume. 





Jobs Board: Careers Online
Find graduate and part time work opportunities on Careers Online, exclusive to current University of Melbourne students. 





Careers and Employability LMS Community 
Access free online learning modules to explore your career options, understand the current job market and prepare your job applications. 





 







Get career advice
We run one-on-one sessions, workshops and student mentoring programs to give you the advice you need, when you need it. Access support from our dedicated team of career advisers.

National Computer Science School (NCSS) Summer School 




About the NCSS Summer SchoolThe National Computer Science School (NCSS) is a ten-day summer school bringing together talented young people from around Australia.NCSS has been offered in various formats since 1996 and in 2024, we will be running concurrently at the University of Melbourne and at the University of NSW (UNSW).All NCSS students reside on campus at a residential college, with NCSS tutors and staff providing pastoral care for the duration of the summer school.If you would like any further information, please email summerschool@grokacademy.org.Connect with usDesigned forNCSS applications will be accepted from students who will complete Year 10 and Year 11 in 2023. No prior knowledge of computing is assumed.WhenSaturday 6th to Monday 15th January 2024WhereNCSS has been offered in various formats since 1996 and in 2024, we will be running concurrently at the University of Melbourne and at the University of NSW (UNSW).All NCSS students reside on campus at a residential college, with NCSS tutors and staff providing pastoral care for the duration of the summer school.Program detailsFor more information on the program, eligibility, selection criteria and to apply see: grokacademy.org/ncssApplication processApplications for January 2024 are closed.If you would like to be notified when applications open for 2025, please visit this link and complete the EOI form.CostThe cost of NCSS is heavily subsidised by our sponsors, allowing us to make the cost per student $500* in 2024 (not including travel).*Financial assistance is available if needed, please enquire on application.EnquiriesIf you have any questions, email summerschool@grokacademy.org.

Alumni and friends




Alumni are valued members of the Faculty of Engineering and IT. From classrooms to worksites, connection is our strength. We encourage you to join this vibrant community of over 40,000 graduates in over 100 countries. Let us celebrate your stories as together we share knowledge, inspiration, support and opportunity.









Support us
Donations help support our students and researchers in their efforts to solve society’s most pressing challenges. Become a valued partner in this endeavour by joining our donor community – creating scholarships, supporting groundbreaking research or helping to build dynamic partnerships.






Graduate stories
Coverage of the varied ways that our graduates are changing the world by combining expertise with passion and vision.






Women in Engineering and IT Alumni Network
Women in Engineering and IT Alumni Network is a group of female alumni and friends that offers support and guidance – helping members and the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology achieve the set mission.






Events
We offer a number of alumni and industry events and networking opportunities, such as reunions and panel conversations with industry members, and researchers, as well as social gatherings and receptions to help you catch up with friends and peers.

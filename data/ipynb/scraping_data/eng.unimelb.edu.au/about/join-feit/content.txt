Education focussed careers






In 2024, the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT) is recruiting 17 Education-focused academics to drive teaching innovation and enhance student learning outcomes. This initiative will significantly increase the Faculty’s cohort of continuing Education-focused academics, reinforcing our commitment to exceptional educational experiences.Successful candidates will make significant contributions to teaching and enhancing the quality of the student experience. Career development opportunities and a clear pathway to Professorship are available.FEIT has a community of Education-focused academics, highlighting our dedication to academic excellence in education. Appointees will collaborate with the Teaching and Learning Laboratory to advance educational practices within our Faculty. Join us in shaping the future of education at FEIT and beyond.

Making an Impact At Australia's leading university our researchers address humanity’s biggest challenges and solve problems for communities around the world. This brings real benefits for the economy, the environment, animal and human wellbeing and health.  Sustainability We have the depth and breadth of research and expertise which enables us to be leaders in the fight against global warming. We will continue to drive change in our campus operations and lead multidisciplinary collaborations and partnerships to address the far-reaching consequences of climate change, empowering our community and inspiring industry-level standards which can be followed by the wider higher education sector.  Priorities and Partnerships Our global collaborations with industry and community enhance our institutional acumen, ensuring sustained leadership for the future. Driving a continued legacy that combines academic excellence with societal impact. Our commitment to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander PeoplesWe aspire to be the university of choice for Indigenous Australians, investing significantly to attract, nurture, and retain Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students and staff. Our Murmuk Djerring strategic priorities — Leadership; Place, Heritage and Culture; Partnerships; Indigenous Knowledge; and Truth-telling and Justice — form the foundation for this commitment. These five priorities empower the University’s Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students and staff to emerge as outstanding leaders addressing contemporary challenges.We're dedicated to enhancing our global reputation in Indigenous research and leadership, expanding academic and student programs. Our commitment extends to inclusive pathways, robust support systems, and the establishment of a dedicated Indigenous Employment Office. To support this commitment, we are also developing a University-wide Cultural Education Program to ensure all of our staff are able to support our vision of fostering a culturally safe, supportive and respectful environment.Discover more about our Indigenous priorities and  commitments:Read more about Murmuk DjerringDownload the Murmuk Djerring PDFOther opportunitiesJobs from affiliated bodies and other opportunitiesStudent employmentCasual employmentResearch Degree OpportunitiesHR@ Unimelb Site footer

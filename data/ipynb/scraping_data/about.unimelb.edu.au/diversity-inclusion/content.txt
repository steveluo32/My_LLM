Disability Inclusion Action Plan The Disability Inclusion Action Plan 2023-2026 represents a significant first step towards achieving the University’s aspiration to become a champion and an exemplar of disability inclusion and accessibility.  LGBTIQA+ Inclusion Action Plan The University’s first LGBTIQA+ Inclusion Action Plan is the result of a consultation process bringing together the lived experience of staff and students, expertise from across the University community, and benchmarking against leading practice.  Pride in Action Network Network of LGBTQIA+ students and staff, their allies and supporters, to share information and foster an inclusive and supportive workplace and learning environment.

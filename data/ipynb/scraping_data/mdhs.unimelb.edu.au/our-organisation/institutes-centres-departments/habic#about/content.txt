Centre for Digital Transformation of Health





 

Connecting digital innovation to healthThe Centre for Digital Transformation of Health at the University of Melbourne imagines a future where the relationship between clinician and patient is guided by clinical data and enabled by digital health technologies.We aim to significantly grow the field of digital health research through creating the right environment for the type of inter-disciplinary, collaborative research that correlates with research excellence and real world impact to flourish.About us

Highlighting Dr Kayley Lyons' Contribution at the 11th Monash University Pharmacy Education Symposium
The Centre for Digital Transformation of Health (CDTH) at the University of Melbourne proudly celebrates Dr Kayley Lyons' participation in the 11th Monash University Pharmacy Education Symposium held at the Monash Prato Centre, Italy, from 7-10 July 2024. This biennial event, themed "Agents of Change," focused on the dual lenses of equity, diversity, and inclusion, as well as digital health, exploring the pivotal role pharmacists have in adapting to crises and optimizing patient care and education.

Dr Mahima Kalla speaks on research methods to make more equitable digital health technologies 
Dr Mahima Kalla presented a seminar in Manchester (United Kingdom) on Wednesday 13 March 2024, as part of the Digital Health Inequities Seminar Series organised by the National Institute for Health Research – Applied Research Collaboration (Greater Manchester area). In her presentation, Dr Kalla shared her experience of drawing from sociological and other multi-disciplinary literature to develop a professional education curriculum on “digital health equity”.

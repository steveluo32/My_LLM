                            Why study at Melbourne?
                         Future focused Meet the needs of the world’s rapidly evolving workplace with a strengthened ability to think critically and creatively across disciplines. Global reach Be part of a vibrant community of students, alumni and academics with strong ties to industry, research and leading universities worldwide. Proven career outcomes We are internationally recognised for graduate employability with unparalleled industry-integrated learning and networking opportunities to set you up for success.Know the career you want but aren't sure how to make it happen? Browse study pathways to common careers. Advancing society Learn alongside leading academics, whose research informs our curriculum, with over 100 research centres, galleries and museums on campus. Direct your own learning experience The Melbourne curriculum enables you to explore your passions and strengths, while giving you the flexibility to specialise when you're ready. Learn how you can create a degree that’s uniquely yours from a choice of over 100 study areas. Explore the Melbourne curriculum  Chevron-right

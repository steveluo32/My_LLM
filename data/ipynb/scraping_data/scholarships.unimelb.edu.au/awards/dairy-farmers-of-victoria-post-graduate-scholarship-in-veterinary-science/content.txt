Summary
The Dairy Farmers of Victoria Postgraduate Scholarship in Veterinary Science is offered to students who are enrolled in the Doctor of Philosophy - Veterinary Science and are undertaking research in a field related to dairy cattle.





Benefits
The scholarship provides a living allowance of approximately $4,500 (depending on the available income) for one year, paid in two instalments of approximately $2,250 in April and September.

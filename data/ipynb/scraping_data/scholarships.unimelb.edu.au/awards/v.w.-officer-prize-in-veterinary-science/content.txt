Summary
The V. W. Officer Prize in Veterinary Science is offered to students who are enrolled in a graduate research degree in Veterinary Science and are undertaking research in the field of diseases and husbandry of sheep and cattle.





Benefits
The scholarship provides a one-off payment of approximately $1000 (depending on the available income from the gift).

Selection criteria
The scholarship is offered to the student(s) ranked highest by the selection panel based on the quality of their application. When determining the successful candidate for this scholarship, other scholarships and prizes received by candidates are considered.
Conditions
Students must agree to the University sharing their name and course details with the donor of their scholarship and provide a note of appreciation or a brief report on the benefits of the scholarship if requested.

Summary
The Behrend Memorial Travel Scholarships is offered to student who are enrolled in the Master of Science in Mathematics and Statistics and are undertaking ravel associated with research and study, including, but not limited to, participation in conferences or workshops.





Benefits
A one-off payment of $1000.

Eligibility and selection criteria
Eligibility
To be considered for this scholarship, you must be enrolled in the Master of Science (Mathematics and Statistics).
Selection criteria
Eligible candidates will be ranked based on:Proposed impact of travel on research and study in the Master of Science (Mathematics & Statistics); andAcademic merit based on Master of Science (Mathematics & Statistics) coursework results.

          Overview
          Over the past decade there has been an explosion in the amount of data captured from mobile phones, the internet, sensors and instruments. Capturing this data is big business, but how do we make sense of it all?That’s where you come in.Learn to integrate and apply statistical and computational principles to solve real-world problems with large-scale data science, and set yourself up for the career of the future.    Related study areas  Computer science Data and analytics Data science Information technology and computer science Mathematics and statistics Science   Chevron-right
